Core Functions

Two essential city services, unified.

Lighting and waste are usually managed separately. SmartStreets brings them together in one elegant urban infrastructure layer.

Adaptive Lighting

Streetlights remain softly lit at idle, then brighten smoothly based on movement, weather, and street activity to improve safety without wasting energy.

Smart Waste Collection

Attached smart bins report fill level and collection urgency, helping operators visit only the bins that need service and optimize city routes.

How It Works

From the street edge to city intelligence.

Input sensors & data

Motion, weather, ambient conditions, and bin fill patterns are captured at street level.

Local edge processing

The pole reacts immediately with lighting adjustments and local monitoring logic.

Cloud AI insights

Aggregated patterns support forecasting, routing, and operational planning.

City dashboard actions

Operators act on recommended lighting strategies and waste collection priorities.

Pilot Context

Designed as a practical Eindhoven pilot.

SmartStreets is framed as a step-by-step concept for Vaartbroek / Dommelbeemd, where lighting and waste services can be tested together in a visible district-scale pilot.
